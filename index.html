Pwnsat is a safe, hands-on platform to learn how space systems like satellites can be hacked, secured, and understood.

Flatsat.org

Simulate

Dive into space mission scenarios with vulnerable firmware and hardware that mirrors real-world satellite systems.

Secure

Learn how to identify risks, apply mitigation techniques, and build more resilient space systems through hands-on exercises.

Explore

Understand the full ecosystem—from radio communication to onboard computers—by experimenting safely in a CTF-like environment.
